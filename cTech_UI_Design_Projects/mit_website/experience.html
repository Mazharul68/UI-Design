experience

Over the years MTI has organized series of foreign study tour exchange program / short term foreign training and medium to long-term Diploma and Masters’ programs, for the officials of government and non-government organizations community leaders, and local people both inside and outside Bangladesh on education, environment, agriculture, health etc. In collaboration with various Universities and MTI’s has arranged several human management training around the world on different pressing issues for the different Ministries of Bangladesh. In addition, there are several national and international organizations where MTI has access for collaboration, if needed to carry out any awarded activities. MTI’s has long experience in organizing training with the expertise of Mahidol University Thailand, Chulalongkorn University Thailand, Ministry of Public Health Thailand, Hanoi Medical University of Vietnam, University of Putra Malaysia, Ministry of Health Malaysia, University of Indonesia, University of Philippine. In the last fiscal year of 2016-17 MTI managed several overseas programs of different ministries and organizations in USA, Italy, Canada, New Zealand, Australia, Brazil, Georgia, Turkey, Morocco, South Korea, China, Thailand, Singapore, Philippines, Indonesia, Sri Lanka, Vietnam and India. Total of 220 nos. (Senior to mid and junior level) government officials of Bangladesh attend the overseas training and study tour program. In the fiscal year of 2014-15 & 2015-16 MTI has awarded to conduct Basic IT/ICT Literacy Training in the district of Sunmaganj under the Sylhet division. From February 2015 to August 2015 MTI has completed 18 Union levels Basic IT/ICT Literacy Training for 360 women those qualification were SSC or HSC or equivalent. From 9 Upazilas (2 unions from each Upzila) of Sunamganj district. Most of the participants were thrilled and enthusiastic to participate in such an innovative training program. In the last fiscal year of 2014-15 MTI managed several overseas programs of different ministries and organizations in USA, Italy, UK, China, Thailand, Malaysia, Laos, Vietnam, India etc. Total of 470 nos. (Senior to mid and junior level) government officials of Bangladesh attend the overseas program. In the fiscal year of 2013-14 MTI managed a series of study tour /training programme in Thailand, Malaysia, Sri Lanka, China etc. under the project of “Strengthening of Bangladesh Public Administration Training Centre” (Phase-III), Ministry of Public Administration, People’s Republic of Bangladesh. A total of 356 nos. Bangladesh government high officials attend the study tour programme. Moreover, MTI organized 15 days long training programme in Sri Lanka under the project of “Strengthening Government through Capacity Development of the BCS Cadre Officials”, Ministry of Public Administration, Dhaka. A total of 100 nos. Bangladesh government high officials were attending the training programme. Furthermore, according to clients need and satisfaction MTI organized different Ministries i.e., Ministry of MOH&FW, Ministry of Agriculture, Ministry of Fisheries & Livestock etc. overseas training and study tour tailor made and customized programme. In the fiscal year 2012-13, MTI organized a series of study tour programme in the Vietnam, Malaysia & Sri Lanka under the project of Strengthening of Bangladesh Public Administration Training Centre” (Phase-III) Project, Ministry of Public Administration, People’s Republic of Bangladesh. A total of 431 nos. Bangladesh government high officials attend the study tour programme. In the fiscal year of 2011-12; MTI organized a significant number of study tour and training programme in the China and India under the project of Strengthening of Bangladesh Public Administration Training Centre” (Phase-III). A total of 451 nos. Bangladesh government entry level, mid-level and high level officials were attended the study tour programme. Moreover, we have also organized 14 days long “Managerial Training Programme” for high level government officials of Bangladesh in the British Geological Survey, Nottingham, United Kingdom. A total of 20 government officials were attending the training programme. Moreover, 5 officials attendded 03 months long Diploma Course in the department of Mineral & Geosciences in the University Kebangsaan Malaysia (UKM), Malaysia. Both were under the Ministry of Power, Energy & Mineral Resources, Department of Geological Survey, Dhaka under the STREC Project. MTI has also organized overseas training program for about 170 participants belonging to MOH&FW to Thailand, Malaysia and China. The government orders related to this assignment are given at as evidence/reference. MTI provided one stop service for the training, study tours and visits organized in different countries for Bangladeshi participants until their return to home land. The services extended include a) identifying and selecting institutes in respective countries b) placement, admission, enrolment of participants, c) estimating costs of training, fees for study tour &visit, d) air tickets, facilitating in obtaining Visa, etc. The participants were from Ministry of Home Affairs, Ministry of Agriculture, Ministry of Fisheries & Live stock, Ministry of Health & Family Welfare (MOH&FW), Government of Bangladesh, Directorate General of Health Services, Bangladesh, representatives from National Nutrition Program (MOH&FW) and Family Planning Departments. The training programs were organized in various renowned universities and training institutions in collaboration with MTI.

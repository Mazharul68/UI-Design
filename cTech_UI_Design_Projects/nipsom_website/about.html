about nipsom:

  • NIPSOM is the apex body in public health in public sector in Bangladesh.
  • One of the largest and oldest post graduate institutes in public health in the South East Asian Region.
  • Largest producer of public health workforce in the country.
  • Established by Father of the Nation Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman (1974); academic program resumed in 1978.
  • Academic courses are affiliated to Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujib Medical University (BSMMU).
  • WHO collaborating centre since 2006.

mission:

To achieve and sustain excellence in every area of its teaching, training, research & consultancy, maintaining and developing its historical position as a world-class institute, and enriching the international, national and regional communities through the fruits of its expertise.

Philosophy:

To become a center of excellence in academic, research and capacity development in public health at international level and lead society towards good health through prevention.

Vision:

Medical education is a way to improve and promote our health and quality of life.

goals:

To conducts studies related to all aspects of ecology related to insect-borne diseases.

objects:

The institute’s commitment of excellence is reflected in its over-arching objectives, set out as below:

  • Teaching: To conduct academic course/programs leading to postgraduate degree (MPhil & MPH) in different disciplines of public health.
  • Training: To organize in-service and continuing education/training programs for different categories of health personnel.
  • Research: To promote and undertake Health System Research as an instruments of public health practice and development.
  • Consultancy: To provide technical advisory/consultancy services in the field of public health.

Departments

Biostatistics, Community Medicine, Entomology, Epidemiology, Health Education, Maternal and Child Health, Microbiology, Nutrition & Biochemistry, Occupational and Environmental Health, Parasitology, Population Dynamics, Public Health and Hospital Administration.

Courses/Programs (9) and Departments (12):

MPhil in Preventive & Social Medicine (24 months, in conventional system), MPH (18 months, in semester system) in Community Medicine, Community Nutrition, Epidemiology, Hospital Management, Health Promotion & Health Education, Health Service Management & Policy, Occupational and Environmental Health, Reproductive & Child Health

Accreditations:

  • Accredited by the University of California (1978), Liverpool University (1982).
  • Training: To organize in-service and continuing education/training programs for different categories of health personnel.
  • Member of institute of International Association of School of Public Health and Asia Pacific Consortium for Public Health (APACPH), 1990.
  • Member of South East Asian Public Health Education Institute Network (SEAPHEIN), 2004.

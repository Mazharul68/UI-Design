Welcome Message

Dear Friends and Colleagues,

Bangladesh Society of Neurosurgeons (BSNS) is going to organize its 10th international scientific conference (11th BSNS-CON) in Dhaka at the Hotel InterContinental from 2-5 June, 2022, with the theme "Navigating the future". We are expecting a good number of eminent neurosurgeons from home and abroad to join us to share and exchange ideas about the recent developments in the field of neurosurgery. We usually have a very pleasant weather in Bangladesh in February, the month of International Mother Language Day. We feel that your stay during the conference will be a very comfortable, enjoyable and memorable one with the academic fiesta. We will be very delighted to welcome you in Dhaka. Please feel free to communicate with us with any query that you may have.

With best wishes and regards

Prof. Kanak Kanti Barua

About Host City Dhaka

The area of Dhaka has been inhabited since the first millennium. The city rose to prominence in the 17th century as a provincial capital and commercial center of the Mughal Empire in South Asia. Dhaka was the capital of Mughal Bengal for 75 years. Since its establishment as a modern capital city, the population, area, and social and economic diversity of Dhaka have grown tremendously. The city hosts over 50 diplomatic missions and the headquarters of BIMSTEC. The city's culture is known for its cycle-rickshaws, cuisine, art festivals and religious diversity.

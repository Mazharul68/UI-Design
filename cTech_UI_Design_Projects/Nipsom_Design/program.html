Name of the Program/Department: Department of Community Medicine

Name of the Program/Department: Department of Community Medicine

Objectives

1. to conduct short courses training for continuous professional development. 2. to conduct academic courses leading to postgraduate degrees in community medicine. 3. to promote and undertake Health System Research (HSR) as instrument of public health practice and development. 4. to promote and undertake Health System Research (HSR) as instrument of public health practice and development. 5. to recognize the concept of community medicine and public health including its evolution, dimension and determinants 6. to conceptualize community as the focal point of the lifetime multidimensional service of physician and provide healthcare according to the felt need of the community and “think globally and act locally”. 7. to identify and prioritize health issues concerning different communities and segments of population in particular. 8. to state the role of different national and international health organizations rendering healthcare services at large including the activities of different healthcare programs addressing the vulnerable groups. 9. to appreciate community health problems with multi professional involvement in a coordinated manner. 10. to identify research priorities in ascending and appraising scientific information, preparing reports and presentation of discussion findings and observations. 11. to recognize patient centered comprehensive public health services including referral, continuing care and follow up.
